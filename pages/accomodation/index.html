Mole Motel - Your Stay at Mole National Park

Mole Motel offers comfortable lodging with stunning views of Mole National Park. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers, the motel gives guests easy access to safari tours, hiking trails, and scenic wildlife views.

Amenities

  • Outdoor Swimming Pool
  • Restaurant and Bar
  • Guided Safari Tours
  • 24-Hour Front Desk
  • Wi-Fi in Common Areas
