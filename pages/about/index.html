About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to the National Parks of Ghana website! Our platform is dedicated to showcasing the beauty, heritage, and unique ecosystems of Ghana’s protected areas. From the lush forests of Kakum to the savannas of Mole, we bring you closer to nature and the adventures waiting in each region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire both Ghanaians and international visitors to explore and cherish the wonders of Ghana’s national parks. By providing comprehensive and accessible information, we aim to promote sustainable tourism, support conservation efforts, and encourage educational engagement with our natural heritage.

What We Offer

  • Detailed information on national parks across Ghana
  • Accommodation recommendations and booking details
  • Guides for activities like safaris, hiking, and birdwatching
  • Visitor guidelines and tips for eco-friendly travel

Join Us on Our Journey

Whether you're a nature enthusiast, an adventurer, or someone looking to relax in a serene environment, our parks offer something for everyone. We’re here to help you plan unforgettable experiences and make the most of your time in Ghana's natural havens.
