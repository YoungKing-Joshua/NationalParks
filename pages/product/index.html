Kakum Canopy Walkway

Experience the thrill of heights

★★★★★

Embark on an unforgettable adventure across the treetops of Kakum National Park. The canopy walkway offers breathtaking views and a unique perspective of the forest.

Adventure Activities

Canopy Walk

Feel the adrenaline as you walk across suspended bridges high above the forest floor. Enjoy panoramic views and spot wildlife from a bird's eye view. The Canopy Walk is an exciting way to appreciate the forest’s upper canopy and gain a new perspective on nature.

Guided Safari Tours

Embark on a guided safari to explore the park’s rich ecosystem. Discover animals such as elephants, monkeys, and colorful birds in their natural habitat, guided by knowledgeable park rangers who share insights into the unique flora and fauna.

Trail Exploration

Discover scenic trails through dense vegetation, encounter diverse flora and fauna, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the park. Trails are available for different skill levels, making it a perfect adventure for families and nature lovers alike.

Nighttime Nature Walks

Experience the thrill of a nighttime walk in the park, listening to nocturnal creatures and the sounds of the forest after dark. The guided night walks offer a unique adventure and a chance to spot nocturnal animals in their natural environment.

Price & Booking

Here are the current prices for our adventure activities:

  • Canopy Walk: GHS 50 for adults, GHS 30 for children
  • Guided Safari Tours: GHS 150 per person (includes guide and vehicle)
  • Trail Exploration: GHS 20 per person (self-guided)
  • Nighttime Nature Walks: GHS 100 per person

Prices may vary on weekends and holidays. Visit our website or contact us for up-to-date pricing, opening hours, and booking options. Plan your visit to Kakum Canopy Walkway today for an unforgettable experience in Ghana's wilderness. Book early to secure a spot for your preferred adventure.
